# Businessgame: Market Domination Grid

A browser-based 9x9 turn-based business strategy game where players compete to dominate markets through investment, expansion, innovation, intelligence, sabotage, and protection.

## Play locally

Open `index.html` in a modern browser.

## Current prototype features

- 9x9 strategic market grid
- Human player versus AI competitor
- Six actions: Invest, Expand, Innovate, Spy, Sabotage, Protect
- Market types: emerging, stable, volatile, regulated, innovation
- Capital, revenue, market share, upgrades, protection and sabotage systems
- Basic AI turn logic
- Victory by reaching 60% controlled market value or by highest value after turn limit
